About Akan Name Generator

Akan name generator is an interesting application which is used to assign names to people using a ghananian naming system that names children based on the day they were born and their gender. It takes in one's date of birth and is able to generate a name for them based on their gender. All you have to do as a user is to enter date of birth which includes day, month and year of birth and it automatically generates an Akan name for you.
